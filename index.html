I am an AI Research Scientist at IBM Research, Israel. My research interests center on evaluating generative AI systems, developing metrics, tools, and frameworks that expose model behavior, failures, and improvement paths. I am particularly interested in evaluation across safety and code-generation tasks, as well as in meta-evaluation approaches that assess the quality of evaluators themselves. I am also very interested in theory of machine learning, dimensionality reduction, approximation and theory of CS in general.

Before joining IBM I was a postdoc at the  CS Department at Aarhus University, hosted by Kasper Green Larsen. I obtained my Ph.D. at the CS Department of the Hebrew University, under the guidance of Yair Bartal and Ofer Neiman .   Email: fandina (at) gmail (dot) com
